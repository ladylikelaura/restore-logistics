Our Story

Built in Arlington. Expanded to Houston. Focused on Texas Freight.

Restore Logistics started with a simple premise: when freight gets rejected, shifted, or stuck, someone needs to step in fast and fix it. We built our business in Arlington, TX around that idea and have been at it for over 25 years.

In 2023 we opened our Houston location to serve the Gulf Coast market and handle the growing freight volume coming through Texas ports and distribution hubs. Two facilities means faster response across more of the state.

We've built vendor relationships on trust and on-time delivery. Every estimate is immediate. Every invoice is same-day. No runaround.
